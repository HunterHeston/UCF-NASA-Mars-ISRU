MAJOR CHANGES in SEE HLA Starter Kit 1.0.1 (14 01 2015)
==========================================================================
GENERAL IMPROVEMENTS:
Kernel
- Added mechanisms to make easy the publishing, subscribing and updating of 'InteractionClass' on the federation execution.
- Resolved an issue when publish two classes related to the same 'ElementObject'. 
- Rewritten some core services related to the publishing, subscribing and updating of 'InteractionClass' and 'ElementObject'.
- Bug fixing.

MAJOR CHANGES in SEE HLA Starter Kit 1.0.0 (03 11 2014)
==========================================================================
GENERAL IMPROVEMENTS:
Kernel
- Added mechanisms to:
	- manage the connection (set-up, hold-up and close up) of a SEE Federate on the federation execution;
	- facilitate the management and the publication of FOMs modules;
	- facilitate the management of the configuration parameters;
	- make easy the publishing, subscribing and updating of 'ElementObjects' on the federation execution.
- Added support for handling the simulation time and related time standard conversions (e.g. JulianDate, MJulianDate, RJulianDate and TJulianDate).
- Added functionalities for subscribing the SEE Reference Frames.
- Added utilities to manage the IP Configuration.
- Improved support for logging service (based on Apache log4j2, website: http://logging.apache.org/log4j/2.x/).
- Added features to check the MS Windows Firewall state.
